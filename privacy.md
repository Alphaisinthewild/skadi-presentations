# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** April 3, 2026

## Overview

This privacy policy describes how OpenClaw and its associated tools ("Services") collect, use, and protect your data.

## Data Collection

We collect:
- **Session data:** Conversations, messages, and interactions within OpenClaw
- **Account info:** Email and authentication credentials
- **Device info:** IP address, browser type, device OS
- **Usage analytics:** Features used, timestamps, session duration

We do **NOT** collect:
- Passwords (OAuth only)
- Payment information (unless explicitly stated)
- Location data
- Camera/microphone access without permission

## Data Usage

Your data is used to:
- Provide and improve the service
- Authenticate and authorize access
- Debug issues and monitor service health
- Comply with legal obligations

## Data Retention

- **Session data:** Retained per your configured settings (default: 30 days)
- **Account data:** Retained until you delete your account
- **Logs:** Server logs retained for 90 days for security purposes

## Data Security

- Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Data at rest is protected with standard security practices
- Access is restricted to authorized personnel only

## Third Parties

OpenClaw may integrate with third-party services (e.g., APIs, OAuth providers). Each integration has its own privacy terms — check their policies.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your data
- Request deletion of your account and associated data
- Opt out of non-essential analytics
- Receive a copy of your data in standard format

## Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, contact the service administrator.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Continued use of the service constitutes acceptance of changes.
